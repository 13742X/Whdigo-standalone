About The Security Model

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

This vault operates on a zero-knowledge principle. All encryption and decryption happens inside your browser. Your Master Password and your data are never sent to a server. We have no ability to access, decrypt, or recover your data.

Argon2id Key Derivation (via WebAssembly)

Your Master Password is fed into Argon2id, considered the gold standard algorithm for password hashing. This runs inside a WebAssembly module for native performance without freezing your browser. Argon2id is a "memory-hard" function, making it extremely resistant to brute-force attacks from powerful hardware like GPUs.

Secure Encryption Standard

Your vault data is encrypted with industry-standard AES-256-GCM. This is a highly secure and widely trusted symmetric encryption algorithm, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of your stored information against all current conventional threats.

Hardened Fragment Storage

Choosing "Save as Secure Fragments" provides maximum security. The vault is split and hidden within a pack of 6 fragments. Only 2 contain real data; the other 4 are encrypted decoys (spoof files). An attacker who steals the files cannot determine which are real, massively increasing the difficulty of an attack.
